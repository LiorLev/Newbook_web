    • Welcome to Newbook Digital Texts in the Humanities

    Directed by scholars, we are an independent publishing house dedicated to enable scholars to make lesser known or understudied literary, historical, and primary texts available quickly and affordably to a wide range of audiences, from specialists to general readers. Our goal is to blend traditional humanities scholarship with dynamic, collaborative, digitally based publishing, and support open access to our texts and technology. All of our materials are available free online, and available for e-readers and print-on-demand.
